About Field 2 Table

Field 2 Table (f2t.io) is a decentralized marketplace designed to connect local food producers with consumers while ensuring compliance with regulations such as Cottage Food Laws. By integrating blockchain solutions for farm shares, we enable producers to sell regulated goods and facilitate out-of-state sales by legally restructuring the transaction model.

Our Features

  • Blockchain Transparency: Immutable records ensuring compliance and trust.
  • NFT-Based Access Control: Dynamic user attributes for decentralized identity management.
  • Scalable Infrastructure: Utilizing AWS RBAC/ABAC for secure access management.
